About Us

At Solid Ground Landworks, we bring beauty, balance, and sustainability to outdoor spaces through thoughtful design and a collaborative process to bring your vision to fruition . We firmly believe that your outdoor space is a place that should be admired and enjoyed all year round.  A place to provide a sense of calm, wonderment and at the same time a space to use for entertaining and hanging out.  Based in Evanston, we specialize in plant design/install, seasonal potted arrangements, hardscaping installations, perennial garden beds, and personalized plant consultations to help homeowners and businesses cultivate stunning, thriving landscapes year-round.

Our Services

Landscape Design & Installation

We work hand-in-hand with you to create outdoor spaces that reflect your vision. Whether that is a more functional backyard, the addition of an outdoor kitchen or firepit, or a more thoughtful landscape that accommodates your unique desires. From concept to installation, we remain focused on the goal of making your space truly yours.

Seasonal Potted Plants & Containers

Lively up your outdoor spaces with beautifully curated seasonal potted plants that can add color and interest during any season, including winter. Our creative plant combinations bring vibrant color and texture to your space, adapting to each season with a wide variety of interesting and unique plant material.

Perennial Beds & Native Plant Gardens

Looking for some more color, texture or a more natural look?  With the right perennial selection your plant beds can provide beautiful interest year after year. We love to incorporate native species whenever possible to support local pollinators and create an eco-friendly landscape. There is nothing like outdoor beauty that can help sustain your own backyard habitat.

Plant Consultation & Garden Planning

Want to start at a slower pace and just figure out where to start and what is possible, or even get your own hands dirty and plant your own plants? We offer hourly plant consultations to guide you in selecting, placing, and caring for plants that thrive in our area. Whether you’re starting fresh or revitalizing an existing garden, we’ll help you make informed choices for lasting beauty.

About Me

Spiro Polomarkakis has been a part of the landscape industry for nearly 20 years.  Starting off as a business manager and account rep at his father-in-law’s business, Moreno Landscaping, in Highland Park, and moving on to found Root&Stem Landscapes, where he focused his efforts on design and install and making sure every client felt educated, in tune and satisfied with their landscape.  Spiro recently started Solid Ground Landworks to focus his full efforts on design and install.  With a deep knowledge and respect for plants, trees and outdoor spaces, Spiro finds joy in educating people and turning their visions into reality.
